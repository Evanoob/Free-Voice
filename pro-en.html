OUR IN-COMPANY TRAINING COURSES

We offer several types of training for professionals, adapting our workshop depending on our audience.

All of our training courses promote dialogue and pro-activity, and are based on active pedagogy in collective intelligence.

From the first minutes, we set the rules to create an atmosphere of humour and empathy, in order to guarantee a serene environment, to ensure everyone is feeling comfortable.

Whatever the chosen format, no academic prerequisites are necessary.

ES'TEAM ‘FREE VOICE’ TRAINING

Aimed at a unit, a department, or any other defined team of which the functioning is based on collaboration, this training aims to enable each person to find their voice within the collective and to promote productive exchanges.

How is this done?

By mobilizing tools from many different fields of study, this training aims to identify the possible obstacles that can hinder the proper conduct of interpersonal exchanges, to deconstruct the patterns that are at the origin of these flawed patterns, and to provide the right tools to establish the best practices.

‘FREE VOICE’ MANAGEMENT TRAINING

Aimed at managers, leaders, and supervisors of an organization, this training aims to provide the necessary tools to establish a climate of trust. This training is based on different workshops. They deliver the tools and reflexes for supervisors to make their team members feel encouraged, so they can give the best of themselves. Thus, avoiding all the pitfalls and risks of unconstructive supervision.

How can this be done?

The tools shared during the workshops are based on methods recommended by several experts worldwide. They come from communication studies, as well as cognitive and behavioral sciences.

MANAGEMENT & ES'TEAM TRAINING

This training is aimed at teams and departments in their entirety, including supervisors. The workshops are opening the dialogue, to reveal the unspoken. Thys, allowing everyone to share their experiences.

How is this done?

A skilful combination of the two training courses mentioned above, it involves the workshop from both of the trainings and additional ones, designed specifically for this particular context.
